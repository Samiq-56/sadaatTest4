Our Mission

This platform was created to assist the Sayyids and Momeneen as a matchmaking service free of cost, strictly adhering to the principle of "Sayyid with Sayyid and Momin with Momin".

  • Real Sadaat o Momeneen are encouraged to join us.
  • We strictly enforce the "Sayyid with Sayyid & Momin with Momin" matrimony policy.
  • Admins are working to launch a dedicated web application based on this ideology.
  • You may post proposals, whether you're a professional matchmaker or client.
  • This service is completely free - never pay anything to anyone.
  • Profiles can be submitted to admins for verification or posted directly.
  • All questions/suggestions about policies must be made privately.
  • Only matrimonial proposals are allowed - no other postings/chats.
  • Admin is not responsible for any incorrect information provided by members.
  • If you are interested in any profile, only the admin will facilitate the exchange of phone numbers between families.
